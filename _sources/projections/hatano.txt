********************************************************************************
Hatano Asymmetrical Equal Area
********************************************************************************

.. image:: ./images/hatano.png
   :scale: 50%
   :alt:   Hatano Asymmetrical Equal Area


.. math::

   \begin{array}

   x &= 0.85\lambda cos \theta

   y &= C_y sin \theta

   P(\theta) &= 2\theta + sin 2\theta - C_p sin \phi

   P'(\theta) &= 2(1 + cos 2\theta)

   \theta_0 &= 2\phi

   \end{array}
